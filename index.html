Episode LXI


They're Coming


Tony2Toes along with his trusted android companion, C3Ptoe, has vanished as he searches for his mentor, Yoghurt. In his absence, the sinister FIRST ORDER has risen from the ashes of the Empire under Baba Feet and Daft Toenail and will not rest until Tony2Toes has been destroyed. With the support of the REPUBLIC, Princess Verruca leads a brave RESISTANCE. She is desperate to find her brother Tony and gain his help in restoring peace and justice to the galaxy. You, our most daring pilot are being sent on a secret mission and join Commander Callus. Your task is to hold back the advance of the imperial Space Feet. You must fight your way through the galaxy, defeat the Space Feet and save Tony2Toes before it's too late. You will be piloting the legendary Starship, Odoureater, and will have to use all your skills to navigate through the treacherous space and defeat the Space Feet. You are our last hope.
